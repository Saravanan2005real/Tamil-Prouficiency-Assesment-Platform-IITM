Principal Investigator and the Research Lead of the Language and Cognition Laboratory

Dr. Anindita Sahoo

Assistant Professor

Indian Institute of Technology Madras

Dr. Anindita Sahoo is the Principal Investigator and the Research Lead of the Language and Cognition Laboratory at the Department of Humanities and Social Sciences, Indian Institute of Technology Madras. Her research explores the intersection of language and cognition through theoretical, typological, and experimental approaches.

At the Language and Cognition Laboratory, she leads investigations into a wide range of linguistic phenomena, understanding how linguistic structures interact with cognitive processes, cultural contexts, and technological applications, with a special focus on Indian languages. Through state-of-the-art experimental facilities and partnerships with leading national and international institutions, the lab advances understanding of language processing and comprehension.

This Language Assessment Platform represents a comprehensive solution developed under her guidance for evaluating language proficiency through advanced AI-driven analysis. The system integrates multiple evaluation modules including semantic similarity, pronunciation analysis, fluency assessment, and coherence detection to deliver precise, objective assessments for Indian languages skills. The first language that has been undertaken through this project is Tamil. Next will be Hindi. And subsequently, other languages will also be added to the list.

The core vision of this project is to assess language ability accurately and inclusively, enabling learners to communicate successfully with their fellow speakers of the same language in the Indian context.
