Language and Cognition Laboratory

The Language and Cognition Laboratory at the Department of Humanities and Social Sciences, Indian Institute of Technology Madras, explores the intersection of language, cognition, and communication through theoretical, typological, and experimental approaches.

Our lab investigates a wide range of linguistic phenomena, understanding how linguistic structures interact with cognitive processes, cultural contexts, and technological applications, with a special focus on Indian languages. Through state-of-the-art experimental facilities and partnerships with leading national and international institutions, we advance understanding of language processing and comprehension.

AI-Powered Unified Tamil Language Proficiency Assessment Platform

This comprehensive assessment platform represents a state-of-the-art solution developed at the Language and Cognition Laboratory for evaluating Tamil language proficiency through advanced AI and natural language processing technologies.

Our system provides objective, data-driven assessments for listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills with detailed analytical insights. The platform integrates multiple evaluation modules including:

  • Semantic similarity analysis for reading comprehension
  • Pronunciation analysis for speaking assessment
  • Fluency assessment and coherence detection
  • Multi-metric scoring system
  • Real-time performance analytics
  • Objective scoring methodology

Every test is designed to give a clear and accurate picture of your proficiency, helping you understand your strengths and areas for improvement. Track your progress over time and gain insights that truly reflect your abilities.

Our Mission

We aim to provide meaningful, accurate, and comprehensive language assessment tools that help individuals understand and improve their Tamil language skills. By leveraging cutting-edge AI technology and linguistic research, we strive to make language proficiency evaluation accessible, reliable, and insightful.
